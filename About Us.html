About Us

Welcome to LOVELOOM Relationship Counseling Services.

At LOVELOOM Relationship Counseling Services, we believe that every relationship has the potential to thrive with the right support and guidance. Our mission is to empower individuals and couples to build stronger, healthier, and more fulfilling connections.



Our Approach

We believe that empathy, understanding, and open communication are the cornerstones of any successful relationship. Our approach is grounded in evidence-based techniques and personalized to fit the unique dynamics of each relationship. We strive to create a safe and supportive environment where clients feel heard, valued, and empowered to make positive changes.



Our Mission

Our mission is to support individuals and couples in their journey towards healthier and more fulfilling relationships. We are committed to providing compassionate care, expert guidance, and effective solutions that foster growth and harmony in relationships.



Our Experts


1. Dr. Rajesh Sagar

Dr. Rajesh Sagar is a Professor of Psychiatry at the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi. He has extensive experience in dealing with relationship issues, mental health, and emotional well-being.
Mission: Dr. Sagar aims to provide comprehensive mental health care, focusing on improving relationships through counseling and therapy. He emphasizes the importance of mental health in fostering healthy relationships.


2. Dr. Rasika Karkare

Ms. Rasika Karkare is the founder of Mind Voyage, an online counseling platform. She holds an M.Sc. in Counseling Psychology from Christ University, Bangalore, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Couple and Family Therapy from TISS, Mumbai.
Mission: Ms. Karkare's mission is to help couples navigate relationship challenges by creating a safe space for communication and understanding. She uses the Gottman Method of Couple Therapy to help couples resolve conflicts and build stronger connections.
