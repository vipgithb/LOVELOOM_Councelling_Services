FAQs (Frequently Asked Question(s))

Question 1 : What types of consultation services do you offer?


Answer : We offer a variety of consultation services, including individual counseling, couples therapy, premarital counseling, and workshops on relationship skills and communication.




Question 2 : How do I book a consultation on your website?


Answer : You can easily book a consultation by visiting our website, selecting the type of service you need, and choosing a convenient date and time from the available slots. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.




Question 3 : What should I bring to a relationship consultation?


Answer : Bring any relevant information or documents that relate to your relationship issues, a list of concerns or questions, and an open mind ready to engage in meaningful discussions.




Question 4 : How long does a typical relationship consultation last?


Answer : Our consultations typically last between 60 to 90 minutes, allowing ample time for thorough discussion and personalized advice.




Question 5 : Is there a fee for the initial consultation?


Answer : Yes, we charge a fee for the initial consultation. The fee structure is outlined on our website, and we offer package deals for multiple sessions.




Question 6 : What should I expect during a relationship consultation?


Answer : During the consultation, the relationship expert will listen to your concerns, ask questions to understand the dynamics of your relationship, and provide tailored advice and strategies to improve your situation.




Question 7 : What if I need immediate help?


Answer : If you need immediate assistance, you can reach out to our support team through our website, and we will do our best to provide timely support or refer you to emergency resources if necessary.
